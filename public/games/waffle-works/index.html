Waffle Works Manual

🧇 Basic Baking▼

Click the giant waffle on the left to manually bake waffles. Spacebar and the 'B' key also work! Accumulate waffles to build your empire.

🏭 Facilities & Upgrades▼

Purchase Facilities from the right panel to automate your Waffles Per Second (WPS) and boost your Waffles Per Click (WPC). Switch to the Upgrades tab to improve their production efficiency or fortify them against disasters.

🐢 Barrier Mode▼

Hungry turtles will invade your field to steal your waffles! Click the grid to perform a Smash Attack and blow them away. Build walls and traps to protect your stash! Your buildings will be placed on the field and turtles can slowly destroy them, dropping their efficiency.

✨ Rebirth System▼

Once you hit the required Waffles and base WPS, you can Rebirth! This wipes your current waffles and facilities but gives a massive permanent multiplier to your production.
